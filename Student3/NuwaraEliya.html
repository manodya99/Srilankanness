Nuwara Eliya
____________________________________

From Kandy, the entrance to the central hills of the island can be reached from Colombo via Nuwara Eliya. Eliya is the most spectacular highland motorcycle in Sri Lanka. Although there is no railway station in Nuwara Eliya, crossing the Highland Railway line makes the journey a pleasant and memorable experience. The journey from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya by train and car is spectacular. The alternate route from Colombo to Nuwara Eliya is through Hatton which is located in the southern part of the central hills.

Nestled at an elevation of 1890 m above sea level, Nuwara Eliya is Sri Lanka's tallest city and is located below the island's highest mountain.   The Spring of the City   For the seasonal event from February to April, Nuwara Eliya has been designated as a sanitary resort in Sri Lanka as well as a foreign tourist. The main events are golf tournaments, horse racing, motor crossings, clay pigeon shooting and carnival events. The disadvantage of this season is that the lodging fees are all too high. With thousands of local tourists arriving in Nuwara Eliya, this season’s budget must be made in advance for overseas tourists due to overcrowding and lack of accommodation.
